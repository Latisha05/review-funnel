
 Workflow

 1. Project Overview

The software is a QR-based AI-powered Review Funnel and Reputation Management platform designed for businesses such as:

* Restaurants
* Cafes
* Car Showrooms
* Hotels
* Salons
* Clinics
* Gyms
* Retail Stores

The main goal is to:

* Increase positive Google reviews
* Capture negative feedback internally
* Improve customer satisfaction
* Automate review collection
* Provide AI-generated review suggestions
* Integrate WhatsApp communication and alerts



 2. Core Concept

Every business receives:

* Unique QR Code
* Unique Review Funnel Link
* Dashboard Access

When customers scan the QR code:

1. They first rate the experience using stars
2. Based on the rating:
   - Positive users are redirected to Google Reviews
   - Negative users are redirected to internal feedback forms
3. AI helps generate review suggestions
4. WhatsApp automations are triggered



 3. High-Level Workflow

Customer scans QR
↓
Landing Page Opens
↓
Customer Gives Star Rating
↓
IF Rating ≥ 4
→ AI Review Suggestion
→ Redirect to Google Review Page
→ Send Thank You WhatsApp

IF Rating ≤ 3
→ Internal Feedback Form
→ Save Complaint
→ Notify Business Owner on WhatsApp
→ Optional AI Recovery Flow

 8. Rating Logic

 Positive Ratings (4⭐ & 5⭐)

Flow:
1. Show AI-generated review suggestions
2. Allow customer to:
   - Copy review
   - Edit review
   - Generate another review
3. Redirect to Google Review page



 Negative Ratings (1⭐–3⭐)

DO NOT redirect to Google.

Instead:
1. Open feedback form
2. Capture complaint internally
3. Notify owner immediately

 26. Important Compliance Notes

The platform should:
* Encourage genuine reviews
* Avoid fake reviews
* Avoid forced review manipulation
* Avoid automatic review posting

System should:
* Redirect satisfied customers to Google
* Capture dissatisfied customer feedback internally

This keeps the system scalable and safer for long-term Google Business compliance.
